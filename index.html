Our History

The government has always recognized the importance of fertilizers in ensuring a thriving agricultural sector. Over the years, multiple subsidy schemes have been introduced to support farmers, but challenges like corruption, unequal distribution, and delays persisted.

To address these concerns, the Subsidized Fertilization Distribution Management System was introduced, leveraging digital technology to provide a transparent, traceable, and accountable method for fertilizer distribution.

Since its launch, the system has helped millions of farmers access fertilizers at subsidized rates, streamlining the process from registration to distribution. This initiative continues to evolve, incorporating new technologies and government policies to better serve farmers nationwide.
